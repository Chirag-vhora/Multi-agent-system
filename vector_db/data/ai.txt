Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines.

There are three main types of AI:
1. Narrow AI
2. General AI
3. Super AI

Narrow AI:
Narrow AI is designed for a specific task. Examples include ChatGPT, Google Maps, and recommendation systems.

General AI:
General AI refers to machines that can perform any intellectual task that a human can do. This type of AI does not exist yet.

Super AI:
Super AI is a hypothetical AI that surpasses human intelligence in every aspect.

Machine Learning:
Machine Learning is a subset of AI that allows systems to learn from data.

Deep Learning:
Deep Learning is a subset of Machine Learning that uses neural networks.

RAG:
RAG stands for Retrieval Augmented Generation.
RAG combines retrieval and generation.

Vector Database:
A Vector Database stores embeddings and helps retrieve relevant information.

Pinecone:
Pinecone is a vector database used in AI applications.

LangChain:
LangChain is a framework used to build AI applications.
